If we want to change the color from left to right then we will have to assign the direction "to right"

syntax: background-image: linear-gradient(to right, color1, color2,.....)

Above you can see that the direction can be changed. for example if you want the gradient from right to left then you will have to type:

background-image: linear-gradient(to left, white, skyblue);
